Who We Are

Welcome to Yachting Services Plus your trusted partner for exceptional yachting services in Tunisia and the Mediterranean. Founded in 2013 by Captain Haithem Dammak — maritime expert, professional captain, and university maritime lecturer — our company brings deep customs and immigration clearance knowledge, hands-on yachting experience, and a love for the sea to every service we provide. Every visit is handled with care, precision, and cultural insight, ensuring captains, crews, and guests receive the support they deserve — from arrival to departure. Whether you’re cruising aboard a motor yacht or a sailing vessel, we deliver seamless, stress-free services crafted specifically for your needs.

Our Services

Customs & Immigration Clearance

Full assistance with arrival and departure formalities, customs procedures, and immigration clearance for yachts entering or leaving Tunisia.

Duty Free Bunkering

Organization and supervision of duty-free fuel bunkering in accordance with Tunisian regulations.

Provisioning

Fresh food, beverages, and technical supplies delivered directly to your yacht, tailored to your requirements.

Additional Yacht Services

Berthing assistance, technical support coordination, crew assistance, and other customized yachting services.

Operating Areas

We assist yachts operating inside Tunisia as well as international yachts requiring support before arrival, during stay, or upon departure.
